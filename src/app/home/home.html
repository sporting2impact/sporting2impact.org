Example Essay: Education Without Price Tags: Why College Should Be Free Introduction: For decades, higher education has been viewed as a pathway to success, innovation, and upward mobility. Yet today, it increasingly functions as a barrier rather than a bridge. The rising cost of tuition forces countless families to take on crushing debt, while others abandon their educational goals altogether. In the United States alone, student loan debt has surpassed $1.7 trillion, leaving graduates burdened for decades. College education should be free for all students because it expands opportunity, strengthens the economy, and represents a critical investment in society’s collective future. Body Paragraph 1: The first and most compelling reason for free college is the way it promotes equal opportunity. At present, a student’s financial background often determines whether they can attend college, how long it takes them to graduate, or whether they graduate at all. Those from wealthier families can pay tuition outright, while others are forced to choose between overwhelming debt and no degree. This dynamic perpetuates inequality and locks many into cycles of poverty. By removing tuition costs, higher education becomes accessible to anyone willing to work for it, regardless of financial status. Countries such as Germany and Finland prove this model works: enrollment rates rise, social mobility improves, and students focus on learning rather than surviving. Body Paragraph 2: The second reason for making college free is its potential to strengthen the economy. A more educated workforce is essential for innovation and competitiveness in the global market. When students graduate without the burden of debt, they are more likely to invest in homes, start businesses, and contribute to the economy. This investment in education can lead to a more skilled workforce, which in turn attracts businesses and creates jobs. By prioritizing education as a public good, society can foster economic growth and stability. Conclusion: In conclusion, making college education free is not just a matter of fairness; it is an investment in the future of society. By promoting equal opportunity and strengthening the economy, free college can help break the cycle of poverty and create a more equitable society. It is time to rethink our approach to higher education and recognize that access to knowledge should not be limited by financial means. Additional Example: A Day in the Life of a Busy Single Mother Introduction: I am a professional single mother, very busy and hard-working with two daughters and demanding work, but I feel fulfilled. My profession and motherhood for my beloved daughters gratify me fully. I begin my day with a good breakfast with my children. By 9:30, I am in my office where I work as a consumer advocate in the state governor's office for children products. Body Paragraph 1: My busy day begins at 6 a.m. in the morning with a big breakfast I prepare for my two daughters (eleven and fourteen years old) and for myself. I spend one good hour with them in the kitchen, eating breakfast and talking with them, and preparing their lunch boxes. This time in the kitchen is very important for me to ensure that my children and I have a family conversation to begin our day, counsel my children for their safety in school and after-school activities. Body Paragraph 2: After an exhausting workday, I do a lot of things to relax, and at the end of the day, I go to sleep happy. My job is demanding, but I find joy in balancing my professional responsibilities with my role as a mother. The support I provide to my daughters and the love we share make every effort worthwhile. Conclusion: In summary, being a single mother and a professional is challenging, but it is also incredibly rewarding. The love and support I give to my daughters, combined with my dedication to my career, create a fulfilling life that I cherish every day. These examples illustrate how to structure a four-paragraph essay effectively, with clear arguments and a cohesive flow of ideas. You can adapt this format to various topics and writing styles as needed.
